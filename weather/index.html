Description

1. Mist: Thin water droplets in the air that make everything look blurry, usually in cool, damp weather.

2. Overcast Clouds: The sky is fully covered with gray clouds, blocking the sun and looking dull.

3. Light Rain: Gentle and soft rain that falls slowly, usually without causing much trouble.

4. Broken Clouds: The sky has gaps of blue visible between patches of clouds.

5. Moderate Rain: Steady rain, not too light or heavy, that can wet roads and surroundings.

6. Heavy Intensity Rain: Very strong and nonstop rain that can cause water to collect on roads.

7. Scattered Clouds: A few clouds spread out in the sky with plenty of blue sky visible.

8. Light Intensity Drizzle: Very fine and soft rain, almost like a mist, barely wetting the ground.

9. Clear Sky: The sky is completely blue with no clouds, giving bright and sunny weather.

10.Drizzle: Very light rain with tiny drops, falling gently and barely wetting the ground.
